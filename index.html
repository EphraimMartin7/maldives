About Us

The Maldives, officially the Republic of Maldives, is an archipelagic state and country in South Asia, situated in the Indian Ocean. It lies southwest of Sri Lanka and India, about 750 kilometres from the Asian continent's mainland The Maldives is a republic in the Indian Ocean. It's made up of a chain of nearly 1,200 islands, most of which are uninhabited. The islands are coral and stand no more than 1.8 meters six feet above sea level. This makes the country vulnerable to rising sea levels associated with global warming.
